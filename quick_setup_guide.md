# 🚀 Quick PDF Generation Guide

## Method 1: Overleaf (FASTEST - 5 minutes)
1. **Go to**: https://overleaf.com
2. **Sign up** for free account
3. **New Project** → **Upload Project**
4. **Upload** `rapport_simple.tex`
5. **PDF appears automatically!**

## Method 2: Local Installation (Windows)

### Step 1: Download MiKTeX
- **Download**: https://miktex.org/download
- **Install** with default settings
- **Restart** your computer

### Step 2: Download TeXstudio (Editor)
- **Download**: https://texstudio.org
- **Install** with default settings

### Step 3: Generate PDF
1. **Open** `rapport_simple.tex` in TeXstudio
2. **Press F5** or click "Build & View"
3. **PDF appears automatically!**

## Method 3: Online LaTeX Editors (Alternatives)
- **ShareLaTeX**: https://sharelatex.com
- **Papeeria**: https://papeeria.com
- **LaTeX Base**: https://latexbase.com

## 📄 What You'll Get
Your PDF will contain:
- ✅ Professional title page
- ✅ Automatic table of contents
- ✅ 20+ pages of structured content
- ✅ All chapters and sections
- ✅ Professional formatting

## 🎨 Customization
Before generating, replace:
- `[Votre Nom]` → Your name
- `[Nom de l'entreprise]` → Company name
- `[Nom de l'encadrant]` → Supervisor name
- `[Nom de l'université/école]` → Your university

## ⚡ Quick Test
If you have LaTeX installed, run:
```
pdflatex rapport_simple.tex
```

The PDF will be generated as `rapport_simple.pdf`
